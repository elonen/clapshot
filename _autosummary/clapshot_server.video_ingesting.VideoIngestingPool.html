clapshot_server.video_ingesting.VideoIngestingPool¶

class VideoIngestingPool(inq, outq, compress_q, db_file, videos_dir, reject_dir, max_workers=0)[source]¶

Bases: MultiProcessor

Orchestrates video ingestion process.

Receives video metadata and compression results, and figures out what do with them.

Stores DB entries, moves files, determines if videos need to be recompressed, and cleans up after errors
